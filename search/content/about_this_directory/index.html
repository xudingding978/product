About Us

The Hospitality Industry Directory is one of a kind in New Zealand, incorporating in-depth information for all sectors of the industry, including equipment, food and beverage, winemaking and print and packaging among others. Magazines covering these industries in print and online are published by WebMedia.


Anyone wishing to conduct business with New Zealand organisations in the sectors covered by our directories, either in print or on-line, will find everything they are looking for here - contact details, key personnel and a full inventory of the products and services offered.


WebMedia

Established in June 2009, WebMedia is a leading publisher of specialist business-to-business publications in traditional print publishing and online services in New Zealand. Our team of creative talent provides expertise in a variety of disciplines including contract publishing services to various organisations, concept development, creative design, writing and editing, print and distribution.
